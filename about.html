Who we are & What we do

Who We Are

Cara is guided by four principles: customer obsession rather than competitor focus, passion for invention, commitment to operational excellence, and long-term thinking. Cara strives to be Earth’s most customer-centric company, Earth’s best employer, and Earth’s safest place to work. Customer reviews, 1-Click shopping, personalized recommendations, Fulfillment by Cara and The Climate Pledge are some of the things pioneered by Cara.

What We Do

Although our business has evolved over the years, one constant is customers’ desire for lower prices, better selection, and convenient services. Today, Cara shoppers can find what they are looking for online and in person. From delivering fresh produce to their doorstep to creating and distributing movies, music, and more, we are always finding new ways to delight our customers.
